That's meaning. Nothing is insignificant. A cup of cold water. A moment of patience. A word spoken at the right time. Small inputs, multiplied by an enormous conversion factor, produce consequences that echo through eternity.

That's faithfulness. Invariant across all perspectives. The same yesterday, today, and forever, regardless of your frame of reference.
